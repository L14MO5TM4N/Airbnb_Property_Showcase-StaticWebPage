Bälinge

Welcome to Bälinge Lakehouse. Escape to our lake house nestled on the serene of Bälinge lake. Enjoy direct access to the lake with a private pier/dock belongs to the house and a rowing boat, perfect for morning swims or fishing. Fully equipped kitchen, fast Wi-Fi, and a fireplace for cozy evenings. 1400 square meter garden, BBQ area. This historical home, over 100 years old, offers a perfect blend of rustic charm and modern comforts.
